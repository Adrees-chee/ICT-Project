About Paris

Paris is the capital and largest city of France, known for its art, history, and architecture. It is a global center for art, fashion, and culture. The city is home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

History

Paris has a history dating back over 2,000 years. It began as a settlement of the Parisii tribe in the 3rd century BC and evolved into one of the most important cities in Europe.
